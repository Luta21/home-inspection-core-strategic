Section Flow — Service Page Conversion Funnel

Each service page needs 8-10 sections that guide the visitor from awareness to action. Which flow feels strongest?

A

Problem-First Flow (Recommended)

Leads with the pain point, then positions the service as the solution. Emotionally driven.

01 Service Hero — Title, one-liner, trust badges, CTA
02 Problem / Risk Section — "Ce se întâmplă dacă NU verifici?"
03 Solution Overview — What this service does & why it matters
04 Methodology Deep-Dive — Step-by-step process with visuals
05 Equipment & Technology — Professional tools used (Flir, sclerometru)
06 Deliverables — What the client receives (report sample, timeline)
07 CTA Banner — Mid-page conversion point
08 Related Services — Cross-sell other inspections
09 Service-Specific FAQ — Address objections
10 Final CTA Section — Phone + WhatsApp + form
B

Authority-First Flow

Leads with credentials and expertise, then explains the service. Trust-driven.

01 Service Hero — Title, credentials, years of experience
02 Trust Strip — Stats: properties inspected, experience, speed
03 Service Overview — Comprehensive description
04 What We Check — Detailed checklist of inspection points
05 Equipment Showcase — Professional tools and certifications
06 Process Timeline — From booking to report delivery
07 Sample Report — Preview of deliverable quality
08 CTA Banner — Conversion point
09 FAQ — Common questions
10 Final CTA — Multiple contact options
C

Story Flow

Follows the client journey — from discovering the problem to getting the solution. Narrative-driven.

01 Service Hero — Cinematic title + real scenario
02 The Scenario — "Imaginează-ți..." storytelling hook
03 The Hidden Risks — What can go wrong without inspection
04 Our Approach — How we solve this, step by step
05 The Tools — Equipment showcase
06 The Report — What you get, sample pages
07 Real Results — Case study / before-after
08 CTA Banner — Conversion point
09 Related Services — Cross-sell
10 FAQ + Final CTA — Close the deal
